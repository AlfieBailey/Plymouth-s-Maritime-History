Plymouth's Maritime Atrractions

With the city being nicknamed as "Britain's Ocean City", it'd be a fairly safe assumption for one to think that there will be a fair share of maritime related attractions scattered around Plymouth, and that is indeed the case. A lot of the notable touristy-like attractions are in the same vacinity as you'd expect, but there are a few maritime hotspots across Plymouth, in particular at/near the coast.
